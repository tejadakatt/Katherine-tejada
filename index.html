Me / My Work

I am an educator. I didn’t really make the connection at first, between my love of teaching children and my excitement at developing and implementing trainings and workshops. I am passionate about teaching not only children but also mentoring and developing adults.
I am dynamic. As the saying goes “A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one.” I have a wide skill set, I can work with a plethora of stakeholders, and I learn quickly because I have experience in different sectors and a diverse pool of colleagues. A reservoir of knowledge gained and transferable from each previous connection made.
I am a leader. In every role that I have had I've been sought out to manage special projects, lead teams to meet deliverables, improve culture, build rapport, and collaborate with partners to surpass goals and bring about productive change.
Above anything else, I am the first-born Hispanic daughter of immigrants. A first-generation college student and family matriarch. I am just a regular girl from The Bronx, NY doing what I can with what I have.
